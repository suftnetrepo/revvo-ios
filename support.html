Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan my notes?

Tap the purple camera button on the home screen. Point your camera at any handwritten or printed notes and tap the scan button. AI will automatically generate flashcards from your notes.

How many free scans do I get?

The free tier includes 10 AI scans per month. Your scan count resets on the 1st of each month. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited scans.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Revvo → Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have Premium access until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → tap the camera button → tap "Restore purchases" at the bottom of the paywall screen.

Does Revvo work with maths and science?

Yes! Revvo supports LaTeX rendering for complex equations in maths, physics, and chemistry. The AI will automatically format equations correctly.

My scanned notes aren't generating good flashcards

For best results: ensure good lighting, hold the camera steady, and make sure the text is clearly visible. Printed text works better than very messy handwriting.
